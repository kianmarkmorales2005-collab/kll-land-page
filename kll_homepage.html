Academics

Programs for
Every Learner

KLL is a public school in Lipa City that provides affordable, quality education across a range of disciplines. Our dedicated faculty combine academic rigor with practical experience, guiding students to succeed both in the classroom and beyond..

As a community focused institution, KLL is committed to making secondary and tertiary learning accessible to every resident of Lipa City and nearby communities, ensuring that education remains within reach for all who aspire to grow.
