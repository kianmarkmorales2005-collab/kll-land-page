Our Story

History &
Mission

Established in 1999, Kolehiyo ng Lungsod ng Lipa (KLL) serves as a cornerstone of higher education in Batangas. It was founded to provide accessible, high-quality tertiary education to the deserving youth of Lipa City, fostering academic excellence and social responsibility.

Over the past two and a half decades, KLL has grown into a thriving academic institution offering a wide range of undergraduate and graduate programs, producing graduates who serve with integrity, lead with purpose, and uplift their communities.

Our Foundation

Vision, Mission &
Goals

Vision

A center of human development committed to the pursuit of wisdom, truth, justice, pride, dignity, local and global competitiveness via quality and free education for all qualified clients.

Mission

Established and maintain an academic environment promoting the pursuit of excellence and the total development of its students as human beings, fear of God, love of country and fellowmen.

Goals

  • Foster the spiritual, intellectual, social, moral, and creative life of its clients via free and quality tertiary education.
  • Provide rich, relevant, and wide-ranging academic disciplines with curricular and co-curricular experiences.
  • Work with government and community in achieving national developmental goals.
  • Develop qualified clients with life skills and prepare them for local and global competitiveness.
